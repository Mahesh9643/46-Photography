"Haldi Cermony - #Painted Yellow"




The Haldi ceremony is a ritual holy bath also known as pithi ceremony, which is one of the pre-wedding ceremonies in India. Turmeric (haldi), oil and water are applied to both the bride and groom by married women on the morning of the wedding. The mixture is believed to bless the couple before the wedding. It is known to have properties that leave the skin fair and glowing. The haldi in some Indian communities is considered auspicious and also signifies protection.Just like any other Indian ritual, the Haldi ceremony too is celebrated with laughter, giggles, pomp and colour!
